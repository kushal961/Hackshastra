Frequently Asked Questions

What is StudentHub?

▶

StudentHub is an online platform that connects students with opportunities, including competitions, networking events, and collaboration spaces.

How do I join a team or create my own?

▶

You can join existing teams or create your own through the "Teams" section, where you’ll find options to connect with other students based on your interests and skills.

Are there any fees for using StudentHub?

▶

StudentHub is free for all students. We aim to make resources and opportunities accessible to everyone.

How can I participate in competitions?

▶

Check the "Competitions" tab for upcoming events and registration details. You can also set reminders to be notified of new competitions.
